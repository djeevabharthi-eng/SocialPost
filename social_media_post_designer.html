Visual Storytelling Principles

1

Lead with emotion

The first 0.5 seconds determine whether someone stops scrolling. Your headline and visual must convey a feeling instantly, before the mind reads a single word.

2

One message per post

Resist the urge to say everything. Each post should do exactly one job — inform, inspire, or invite action — and do it clearly.

3

Consistent brand identity

Use the same font pairing, color palette, and layout structure across posts. Recognizability builds trust over time — people should spot your brand before reading the handle.

4

Design for the feed, not the canvas

Your post will be seen at thumbnail size, mid-scroll, on a phone screen. Test legibility at small sizes and ensure the core message reads in under 2 seconds.
